What is the Employment Gold Card?

Taiwan Employment Gold Card is a special visa launched in 2018 to attract “special” talents in Taiwan. It gives you the right to stay and work in Taiwan from 1 year up to 3 years (renewable). You’ll be able to work for any company, start a new business, have great tax incentives, and benefit Taiwan healtchare system. Your dependants will also be able to follow you.
